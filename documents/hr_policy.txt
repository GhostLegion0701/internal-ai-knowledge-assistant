HR Policy Handbook

Employees are expected to maintain professional behavior at all times during working hours and while representing the company externally.

The standard working hours are 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday to Friday. Employees are allowed flexible arrival within 30 minutes with manager approval.

All employees must complete mandatory compliance and workplace conduct training within 30 days of joining.

Performance reviews are conducted twice a year, in June and December.

Employees should contact the HR team for questions related to onboarding, code of conduct, workplace issues, and policy clarification.
