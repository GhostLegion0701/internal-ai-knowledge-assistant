Leave Policy

Full-time employees are entitled to 18 paid leave days per calendar year.

Employees may also take up to 10 sick leave days per year. Sick leave beyond 2 consecutive days may require a medical certificate.

Unused paid leave can be carried forward up to a maximum of 5 days into the next calendar year.

Leave requests must be submitted through the HR portal at least 3 working days in advance unless it is an emergency.

For parental leave and other special leave categories, employees should speak directly with HR.
